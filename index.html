WELCOME TO SWAMI SUKHDEVANAND COLLAGE

Swami Shukdevanand Postgraduate College is a leading educational institution of higher learning in Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh. The college is located at Mumuksh Ashram, near Garra river on Lucknow-Delhi National Highway No. 24. It is affiliated to MJP Rohilkhand University, Bareilly. Founded in 1964 by Swami Shukdevanand Saraswati Ji Maharaj, SSPG College at present runs graduate and postgraduate courses in Faculties of Arts, Science, Commerce, Education and Computer Science. With the dedication of teachers and non-teaching staff, the college is establishing newer heights in quality education.
